## 🎯 CONDITION-SPECIFIC PROTOCOLS

### POST-TPLO/ACL SURGERY
**Week 1-2:** PROM_STIFLE, COLD_THERAPY, MASSAGE_THERA, EFFLEURAGE  
**Week 3-4:** Add SIT_STAND, WEIGHT_SHIFT, SLOW_WALK, UNDERWATER_TREAD  
**Week 5-6:** Add BALANCE_PAD, CAVALETTI_RAILS, HILL_CLIMB (gentle)  
**Week 7-8:** Add WOBBLE_BOARD, THREE_LEG_STAND, STAIR_CLIMB  
**Week 9-12:** Add BOSU_STAND, RESIST_WALK, TROT_CIRCLES  
**Modalities:** TENS_THERAPY, NMES_QUAD, US_PULSED (early), LASER_IV

### HIP DYSPLASIA
**Core Exercises:** PROM_HIP, STRETCH_QUAD, UNDERWATER_TREAD, POOL_SWIM  
**Strengthening:** HILL_CLIMB, BACKWARD_HILL, RESIST_WALK, STAIR_CLIMB  
**Balance:** BALANCE_PAD, WOBBLE_BOARD, SLOW_PIVOT  
**Modalities:** HEAT_THERAPY (before exercise), US_CONTINUOUS, LASER_IV  
**Manual:** MYOFASC_ILIO, JOINT_MOB_G1-G2

### NEUROLOGICAL (IVDD, FCE)
**Early:** PROM exercises, UNDERWATER_TREAD (low level), MASSAGE_THERA  
**Proprioception:** FOAM_PAD_STAND, SURFACE_CHANGE, CAVALETTI_RAILS  
**Coordination:** BLINDFOLD_WALK, PERTURBATION, LASER_CHASE  
**Advanced:** FIGURE8_CONE, DIAGONAL_LIFT, PLATFORM_TRANS  
**Modalities:** NMES_QUAD (prevent atrophy), LASER_IV, PEMF_THERAPY

### CHRONIC PAIN/OA
**Daily:** HEAT_THERAPY, MASSAGE_THERA, EFFLEURAGE, COLD_THERAPY (after)  
**Gentle Exercise:** SLOW_WALK, UNDERWATER_TREAD, WATER_WALKING  
**ROM Maintenance:** PROM exercises, STRETCH_QUAD, STRETCH_HAMS  
**Modalities:** TENS_THERAPY, LASER_IV, HYDRO_WHIRL, CONTRAST_BATH  
**Manual:** All JOINT_MOB grades, MYOFASC releases

## ⚠️ RED FLAGS - STOP EXERCISE

- **Pain:** Vocalization, limping, reluctance to continue
- **Swelling:** Increased joint effusion or edema
- **Heat:** Excessive warmth at surgical/injury site
- **Lameness:** Grade 3/5 or worse during exercise
- **Fatigue:** Excessive panting, difficulty standing
- **Behavior:** Aggression, severe anxiety, withdrawn

## 📊 PROGRESSION GUIDELINES

### 10% Rule
Increase duration, intensity, or difficulty by max **10% per week**

### Phase Progression
**Phase 1 (Acute):** Passive therapy, pain control (1-2 weeks)  
**Phase 2 (Subacute):** Active assisted, gentle ROM (2-4 weeks)  
**Phase 3 (Strengthening):** Progressive loading (4-8 weeks)  
**Phase 4 (Functional):** Sport-specific, ADL training (8-12 weeks)  
**Phase 5 (Maintenance):** Ongoing fitness (12+ weeks)

### Frequency
- **Passive Therapy:** 2-3x daily
- **Active Assisted:** 2x daily, 7 days/week
- **Strengthening:** 1-2x daily, 5-6 days/week
- **Balance/Proprioception:** 1x daily, 5 days/week
- **Aquatic:** 3-5x weekly
- **Modalities:** Per device protocol, typically daily
