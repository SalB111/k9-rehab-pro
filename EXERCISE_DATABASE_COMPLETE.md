## 📋 EXERCISE STRUCTURE

Each exercise contains the following comprehensive information:

1. **code**: Unique identifier (e.g., "UWTT_FORWARD")
2. **name**: Descriptive name
3. **category**: Clinical category
4. **description**: Clinical rationale and purpose
5. **equipment**: Required materials (array)
6. **setup**: Initial positioning and preparation
7. **steps**: Detailed step-by-step instructions (array)
8. **good_form**: Proper execution criteria (array)
9. **common_mistakes**: Errors to avoid (array)
10. **red_flags**: Warning signs requiring intervention (array)
11. **progression**: How to advance difficulty
12. **contraindications**: When NOT to perform
13. **difficulty_level**: Easy/Moderate/Advanced
