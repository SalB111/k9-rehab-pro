## 🎯 CONDITION-SPECIFIC PROTOCOLS (VETERINARY-ACCURATE)

### **TPLO POST-OP (8-16 weeks):**

**Phase 1 (Weeks 0-2): Acute/Inflammatory**
- TENS for pain
- Cryotherapy post-op
- PROM stifle (gentle)
- Laser therapy
- Ice massage
- Compression therapy

**Phase 2 (Weeks 3-6): Proliferation**
- NMES quadriceps (prevent atrophy)
- Heat before PROM
- Underwater treadmill (deep water, high buoyancy)
- Joint mobilization Grade I-II
- Sit-to-stands
- Slow leash walks
- Weight shifting

**Phase 3 (Weeks 7-12): Remodeling**
- Cavaletti rails
- Hill climbing (incline)
- Underwater treadmill (shallow, more resistance)
- Incline treadmill 5%
- Joint mobilization Grade III
- Balance pads
- Three-leg standing
- Platform step-ups (low)

**Phase 4 (Weeks 12-16): Return to Function**
- Decline treadmill (eccentric control)
- Incline treadmill 10%
- Platform step-ups (high)
- Trotting intervals
- Figure-8s
- Sport-specific training
- Plyometrics (if athletic dog)

---

### **HIP DYSPLASIA (Chronic Management):**

**Early Stage:**
- Heat therapy
- Underwater treadmill (buoyancy support)
- PROM hip
- Iliopsoas myofascial release
- Gentle swimming
- Weight management exercises

**Strengthening Phase:**
- Hip abductor strengthening (resistance bands)
- Hill climbing
- Lateral walking
- Balance training
- Core strengthening

---

### **CCL (Non-Surgical Conservative Management):**

**Phase 1 (Weeks 0-4):**
- Strict rest
- Cryotherapy
- TENS
- PROM (gentle)
- Underwater treadmill (high buoyancy)

**Phase 2 (Weeks 4-8):**
- NMES quadriceps
- Joint mobilization
- Aquatic therapy progression
- Controlled walking
- Weight shifts

**Phase 3 (Weeks 8-16):**
- Progressive strengthening
- Balance training
- Proprioception work
- Controlled return to activity

---

### **IVDD (Spinal Cases):**

**Conservative Management:**
- Laser therapy
- Heat therapy
- Myofascial release
- Core strengthening
- Balance training (critical for proprioception)
- Slow, controlled exercises
- Avoid jumping/stairs initially

**Post-Surgical:**
- Similar to conservative but more aggressive timeline
- Early assisted standing
- Wobble board work
- Underwater treadmill (support)
- Progressive weight-bearing
