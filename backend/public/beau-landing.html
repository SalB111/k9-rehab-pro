Products & features

What B.E.A.U. does.

Five interlocking clinical capabilities. All evidence-based. All ACVSMR-aligned. All exclusive to K9 Rehab Pro.

Biometric patient intake

Captures species, breed, age, body weight, BCS, condition, pain score, ROM deficit, gait grade, and muscle atrophy classification before generating any protocol output.

AI protocol generation

Generates a fully individualized, phase-appropriate exercise protocol from the verified library — matched to condition, recovery phase, and patient biometrics.

Evidence grading

Every protocol output includes an evidence confidence score. Exercises are selected from the Easy / Moderate / Advanced taxonomy aligned to Millis & Levine criteria.

Phase-based progression

Protocol outputs are phase-structured. B.E.A.U. maps exercises to the correct rehabilitation phase — acute, subacute, or active recovery — with defined progression milestones.

Clinical dashboard integration

B.E.A.U.-generated protocols link directly to the patient record. Track adherence, pain score trends, ROM improvement, and phase transitions across 113 active protocols.

ACVSMR protocol coding

52 verified protocol codes map B.E.A.U. outputs to ACVSMR-recognized clinical conditions. Documentation from day one aligns with the governing body for veterinary rehabilitation.

Safety standards

Taking clinical safety seriously.

Applying AI to veterinary rehabilitation requires the highest standards of clinical validity. B.E.A.U. is not a general-purpose language model — it is a domain-specific clinical decision support system with strict boundaries.

B.E.A.U. operates in strict verification mode. Clinical accuracy is non-negotiable and takes priority over speed or convenience. Every output is grounded in evidence, not probability.

No hallucinated clinical content — all outputs are drawn from the verified 223-exercise library

ACVSMR protocol codes provide an auditable clinical reference for every generated protocol
