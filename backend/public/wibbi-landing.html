AI clinical decision support

Boost clinic efficiency with B.E.A.U.

B.E.A.U. — the Biomedical Evidence-Based Assessment Utility — is the only AI system built specifically for veterinary rehabilitation. Load a patient, describe their condition, and receive a fully individualized, ACVSMR-aligned protocol in under 60 seconds.

  • Generate evidence-based protocols from 235 exercises across 14 clinical categories
  • Condition-specific decision trees for 13 diagnoses — TPLO, IVDD, DM, hip DJD, lumbosacral, and more
  • Phase-gate criteria with objective thresholds in degrees and centimeters, not vague descriptions
  • Hydrotherapy dosing — water depth, weight-bearing %, speed by phase, temperature, frequency

Clinical intake & goniometry

Objective measurements that feed directly into B.E.A.U.

K9 Rehab Pro's intake form captures goniometric ROM for the affected and contralateral limb, with live asymmetry calculation and per-joint Millis & Levine normal reference values. Every measurement goes directly into B.E.A.U.'s patient context — so protocols are anchored to real clinical data.

  • ROM goniometry: affected and contralateral flexion/extension with live deficit calculation
  • Per-joint normal reference values — Stifle, Hip, Elbow, Shoulder, Hock, Carpus
  • Color-coded severity: minimal / moderate / severe with phase-gate guidance
  • Limb circumference bilateral with atrophy classification and asymmetry %
  • All measurements stored to patient record and injected into every B.E.A.U. session
