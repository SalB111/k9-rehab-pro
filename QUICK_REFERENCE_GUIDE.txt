🦴 ORTHOPEDIC CONDITIONS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Hip Dysplasia:
  → Start: PROM_HIP, MASSAGE_THERA, HEAT_THERAPY
  → Progress: WATER_WALK_SHALLOW, SIT_TO_STAND, CAVALETTI_RAILS
  → Advanced: HILL_CLIMB, POOL_SWIM, BALANCE_DISC

  → Week 1-2: POST_TPLO_WEEK1, PROM_STIFLE, COLD_THERAPY
  → Week 3-4: ASSISTED_STANDING, WATER_WALK_SHALLOW, GENTLE_PROM
  → Week 5-8: SIT_TO_STAND, CAVALETTI_RAILS, POOL_SWIM
  → Week 9+: POST_TPLO_WEEK4, HILL_CLIMB, AGILITY_PREP

  → Early: POST_FHO_EARLY, PROM_HIP, MASSAGE_THERA
  → Mid: ASSISTED_STANDING, WATER_WALK, COOKIE_STRETCH
  → Late: POST_FHO_LATE, BALANCE_DISC, STRENGTHENING

📋 TEMPLATE 1: POST-SURGICAL TPLO (12-Week Protocol)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WEEK 1-2 (Easy only - 5-10 min sessions):
  • POST_TPLO_WEEK1 (specific protocol)
  • PROM_STIFLE (10 reps, 2x daily)
  • COLD_THERAPY (15 min post-activity)
  • LASER_THERAPY (3x week)
WEEK 3-4 (Easy-Moderate - 10-15 min sessions):
  • ASSISTED_STANDING (3 sets x 30 sec)
  • WATER_WALK_SHALLOW (chest depth, 5 min)
  • GENTLE_PROM (full range, no force)
  • SIT_TO_STAND (5 reps, 2x daily)
  • THERAPEUTIC_US (alternate with laser)

WEEK 5-8 (Moderate - 15-20 min sessions):
  • SIT_TO_STAND (10 reps, 2x daily)
  • CAVALETTI_RAILS (5 passes, rail height 2-3")
  • POOL_SWIM (5-7 min, controlled)
  • BALANCE_DISC (30 sec holds, 3 reps)
  • UNDERWATER_SIT (water support for joint)

WEEK 9-12 (Moderate-Advanced - 20-30 min sessions):
  • POST_TPLO_WEEK4 (advanced protocol)
  • HILL_CLIMB (gentle slope, 100 feet)
  • AGILITY_PREP (low jumps, basic weaves)
  • POOL_SWIM (10-15 min intervals)
  • PERTURBATION (balance challenges)

📋 TEMPLATE 2: GERIATRIC MOBILITY PROGRAM (Ongoing)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  • SENIOR_WALK (flat surfaces, slow pace)
  • GENTLE_PROM (all major joints)
  • SENIOR_MASSAGE (focus on stiff areas)

3X WEEK (10-15 min):
  • HEAT_THERAPY (15 min pre-activity)

2X WEEK (Professional):
  • ACUPUNCTURE (licensed vet only)
  • LASER_THERAPY (Class IV, targeted)

📋 TEMPLATE 3: SPORT DOG PRE-COMPETITION (8-Week Cycle)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PHASE 1 - BASE CONDITIONING (Weeks 1-3):
  MON: ENDURANCE_LONG (30 min jog) + POOL_SWIM (15 min)
  TUE: RESIST_WALK (10 min) + CAVALETTI_RAILS (10 passes)
  WED: Rest or MASSAGE_THERA + GENTLE_PROM
  THU: HILL_CLIMB (20 min) + BALANCE_DISC (5 min)
  FRI: AGILITY_PREP (20 min) + STRETCHING_PROTO
  SAT: POOL_SWIM (20 min) + LASER_THERAPY
  SUN: Rest

PHASE 2 - STRENGTH & POWER (Weeks 4-6):
  MON: SPRINT_INTERVAL (8x30sec) + WEIGHT_PULL (light)
  TUE: JUMP_GRIDS (15 min) + POLE_WEAVE (10 min)
  WED: Active recovery - WATER_WALK + MASSAGE
  THU: RESIST_WALK (15 min) + TROT_CIRCLES (10 min)
  FRI: AGILITY_WEAVE (specific) + FLYBALL_COND
  SAT: DISC_DOG drills + STRETCHING_ADV
  SUN: Rest + CHIROPRACTIC (if needed)

PHASE 3 - COMPETITION PEAK (Weeks 7-8):
  MON: Light AGILITY_PREP (skill work only)
  TUE: POOL_SWIM (recovery) + LASER_THERAPY
  WED: Rest + MASSAGE_DEEP
  THU: Brief skill work (10 min) + STRETCHING
  FRI: Rest or very light movement
  SAT: Competition
  SUN: COLD_THERAPY + MASSAGE + Rest

📋 TEMPLATE 4: NEUROLOGICAL RECOVERY - IVDD (16-Week Protocol)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ACUTE PHASE - Weeks 1-2 (Medical management primary):
  • NEURO_PROM (gentle, 2-3x daily)
  • MASSAGE_THERA (light touch only)
  • COLD_THERAPY (if inflammation present)
  • No active exercise until vet clearance

EARLY RECOVERY - Weeks 3-6:
  • NEURO_PROM (full range, 3x daily)
  • NEURO_TACTILE (sensory stimulation)
  • ASSISTED_STANDING (3 sets x 20-30 sec)
  • WATER_WALK_SHALLOW (buoyancy support, 3-5 min)
  • LASER_THERAPY (nerve regeneration, 3x week)

MID RECOVERY - Weeks 7-10:
  • NEURO_COORD (coordination drills)
  • CAVALETTI_RAILS (very low, proprioception)
  • BALANCE_DISC (assisted, 30 sec holds)
  • POOL_SWIM (controlled, 5-7 min)
  • UNDERWATER_SIT (practice motor patterns)

LATE RECOVERY - Weeks 11-16:
  • NEURO_GAIT (advanced gait training)
  • VESTIB_TRAIN (if balance affected)
  • PERTURBATION (challenge stability)
  • POOL_SWIM (10-15 min intervals)
  • AGILITY_PREP (only if fully recovered)

                     CONTRAINDICATION QUICK CHECK
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

⛔ ABSOLUTE CONTRAINDICATIONS (DO NOT PROCEED):
  • Active infection
  • Uncontrolled pain
  • Unstable fracture
  • Acute hemorrhage
  • Severe cardiac disease (for intensive exercise)
  • Fever > 102.5°F
  • Open wounds (for water therapy)
  • Severe respiratory distress

⚠️  RELATIVE CONTRAINDICATIONS (Modify or Postpone):
  • Recent surgery (timing depends on procedure)
  • Acute inflammation
  • Grade 4+ lameness
  • Seizure activity
  • Severe fear or aggression
  • Extreme weakness
  • Joint effusion
  • Unhealed incisions

✅ WHEN TO PROGRESS:
  • Pain-free during current exercises
  • Consistent good form
  • No increased lameness post-exercise
  • Veterinary approval obtained
  • Owner compliance demonstrated
  • Adequate recovery between sessions

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                     EXERCISE FREQUENCY GUIDELINES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

📅 PASSIVE THERAPY:
  • Frequency: 2-3x daily
  • Duration: 5-10 minutes per session
  • Can be done at home by owner

📅 ACTIVE ASSISTED:
  • Frequency: 2-3x daily
  • Duration: 5-15 minutes
  • Requires initial supervision, then home exercise

📅 STRENGTHENING:
  • Frequency: 3-5x per week
  • Duration: 15-30 minutes
  • Allow 24-48 hours recovery between intense sessions

📅 BALANCE/PROPRIOCEPTION:
  • Frequency: 3-5x per week
  • Duration: 10-20 minutes
  • Can alternate with strengthening days

📅 HYDROTHERAPY:
  • Frequency: 2-3x per week
  • Duration: 10-20 minutes in water
  • Professional supervision required initially

📅 THERAPEUTIC MODALITIES:
  • Frequency: 2-3x per week
  • Duration: 15-30 minutes
  • Requires professional equipment/training

📅 SPORT CONDITIONING:
  • Frequency: 4-6x per week (competitive athletes)
  • Duration: 30-60 minutes
  • Include rest days

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                     RED FLAGS - STOP EXERCISE IMMEDIATELY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🚨 STOP IF YOU SEE:
  • Acute pain response (vocalizing, guarding)
  • Sudden onset lameness
  • Collapse or severe weakness
  • Respiratory distress
  • Aggressive behavior change
  • Joint swelling within 2 hours post-exercise
  • Vomiting or diarrhea during exercise
  • Trembling or muscle cramping
  • Disorientation or ataxia
  • Refusal to bear weight

📞 CONTACT VETERINARIAN IF:
  • Red flags persist > 24 hours
  • Swelling increases
  • Lameness worsens
  • Pain not controlled with prescribed medications
  • New symptoms develop

                     DOCUMENTATION & TRACKING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

📝 RECORD FOR EACH SESSION:
  • Date and time
  • Exercises performed (codes)
  • Repetitions/duration
  • Difficulty level
  • Patient tolerance (1-10 scale)
  • Any adverse reactions
  • Progress notes
  • Owner observations at home

📊 WEEKLY ASSESSMENTS:
  • Pain score (0-10)
  • Lameness grade (0-5)
  • Range of motion measurements
  • Muscle girth measurements (affected limbs)
  • Weight (geriatric and weight loss patients)
  • Functional ability (stairs, jumping, etc.)

📈 MONTHLY EVALUATION:
  • Comprehensive physical exam
  • Progress toward goals
  • Treatment plan adjustments
  • Client satisfaction
  • Home exercise compliance
